Artificial intelligence-driven robotic charging of electric vehicles: from vision-based pose estimation to robust compliant docking

Abstract

A novel vision-based robotic EV charging system is proposed that combines deep learning-based keypoint detection with compliant control for robust plug insertion. The system operates under significant pose uncertainty and demonstrates reliable performance in real-world conditions.

Experiment 2 - Jamming-aware adaptive compliant insertion strategy

Results

The proposed methods achieve high success rates even under large initial pose errors. The compliant control strategy ensures safe and stable insertion, reducing mechanical stress and failure cases.
